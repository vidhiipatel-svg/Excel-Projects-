An interactive Excel-based Member Information System (MIS) dashboard designed to monitor and analyze gym membership retention and performance metrics. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into member behavior through three integrated sheets: a visual dashboard with key performance indicators, chart data for analytics, and detailed member profiles.
Key features include member segmentation by status (Active, At Risk, Lapsed, New), membership plan analysis with revenue tracking in CAD, check-in monitoring by month, and popular class identification. The system tracks 60 members with detailed demographics including age, gender, join dates, renewal timelines, payment status, and visit frequency.
The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by identifying retention risks through days-since-visit metrics, analyzing revenue patterns across different membership tiers, evaluating marketing channel effectiveness through lead source tracking, and monitoring class popularity. This tool empowers gym management to proactively engage at-risk members and optimize operational strategies.
